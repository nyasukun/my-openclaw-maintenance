# Proposal Writing

Use this skill when producing proposals or structured business drafts.

Prefer this structure:

- objective
- current situation
- proposed approach
- deliverables
- assumptions and exclusions
- timeline or next steps

Keep claims defensible and mark placeholders clearly.
